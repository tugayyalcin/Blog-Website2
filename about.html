Who Is Tugay Yalçın

Tugay Yalçın was born on 26 April 2002 in Istanbul. He graduated from Yahya Kemal Beyatlı Anatolian High School in Bakırköy. Currently, Software Engineering at Istanbul Sabahattin Zaim University, he is a second grade student.

Tugay Yalçın's Skills and Hobbies

Tugay Yalçın is a native speaker of Turkish and speaks English and German at B2 level.
Tugay Yalçın, who has achieved many successes in national and international chess tournaments, is currently the president of Istanbul Sabahattin Zaim University Chess Club and provides training to students who want to learn chess and organizes tournaments.
One of Tugay Yalçın's future goals, who is interested in history, philosophy and politics, is to improve himself in social sciences as well as in the software industry.
